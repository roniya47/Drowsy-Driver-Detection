Tips

Get Adequate Sleep:

Ensure you are well-rested before driving to prevent fatigue.

Take Regular Breaks:

Schedule breaks to rest and stretch during long drives.

Stay Hydrated:

Drink water to stay alert, as dehydration can lead to fatigue.

Avoid Heavy Meals:

Opt for lighter snacks to prevent drowsiness caused by a full stomach.
